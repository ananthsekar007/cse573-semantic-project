=== ABSTRACT ===
The invention discloses a wind-solar hybrid power generation system which comprises a wind power generation system, a solar power generation system and a rainwater collection system. The wind power generation system is used for converting mechanical energy into electric energy and storing the electric energy in the storage battery; the solar power generation system is used for converting solar radiation energy into electric energy through a photoelectric effect and storing the electric energy in the storage battery; when raining, the rainwater collection system collects rainwater that falls on the system. This power generation system includes wind power generation, solar energy power generation and the combination of rainwater collection function, and system simple structure generates electricity green safety, and electricity generation economic benefits is high, builds at high-rise building top, and the geometry depends on the building structure profile, both can provide the green energy for the building, can not cause negative visual effect again, can also eliminate the worry to the bird problem of hitting, improves the security and reduces the vibration. The system can also be used in urban areas and remote areas with low wind speed and high wind speed, and has strong practicability.

=== CLAIMS ===
  1. A wind-solar hybrid power generation system, comprising:
      a battery for storing electrical energy;
      the wind power generation system comprises a wind turbine, a power turbine guide vane and a generator, wherein the wind turbine is positioned in the middle of the device and surrounded by the power turbine guide vane, and a central driving shaft of the wind turbine is connected with the generator; the generator is used for converting mechanical energy into electric energy and storing the electric energy through the storage battery; the power turbine guide vane comprises an upper wall guide plate, a lower wall guide plate and guide blades, wherein the upper wall guide plate and/or the lower wall guide plate are conical plates, the upper wall guide plate and the lower wall guide plate are opposite to each other in an up-down mode and are provided with openings facing outwards, the upper wall guide plate and the lower wall guide plate are connected through a plurality of guide blades, and the guide blades are arranged in a circumferential array;
      the solar power generation system comprises a solar panel, the solar panel is mounted on the top surface of the upper wall guide plate, and the solar panel is obliquely arranged; the solar panel is used for converting solar radiation energy into electric energy through a photoelectric effect and storing the electric energy through the storage battery;
      the rainwater collecting system is used for collecting rainwater falling on the system;
      the wind turbine and the power turbine guide vane are taken as a structural unit, and a plurality of structural units can be vertically stacked.
  2. The wind-solar hybrid power generation system according to claim 1, wherein the rainwater collection system comprises a rainwater filter, a rainwater passage, a water storage tank and a control switch; the rainwater channel is positioned below the solar cell panel; the top of the rainwater channel is connected with the rainwater filter, and the bottom of the rainwater channel is connected with the water storage tank; the control switch is arranged on the water storage tank and used for controlling the drainage of rainwater in the water storage tank.
  3. The wind-solar hybrid power generation system of claim 1, wherein the wind turbine is a vertical axis wind turbine; the power turbine guide vane is a fixed guide vane mechanism or an adjustable guide vane mechanism.
  4. The wind-solar hybrid power system according to claim 1, wherein the solar panel is a solar photovoltaic or solar thermal panel.
  5. The wind-solar hybrid power generation system of claim 1, wherein the bottom of the solar panel is mounted by a gimbal structure; the solar power generation system further comprises an intelligent controller, and the intelligent controller can automatically control the solar panel to pivot in a universal mode by detecting the illumination direction and/or the illumination intensity.
  6. The wind-solar hybrid power generation system of claim 1, further comprising a mesh enclosure, wherein the mesh enclosure is sleeved on the outermost side of the system and is used for preventing foreign objects from impacting the wind turbine.
  7. The complementary wind and solar power generation system of claim 1, wherein the system is adapted to be built on top of a building or structure and is provided with mounting and locking means.

At present, there are many types of solar power generation technologies. The existing solar power generation devices are mainly composed of solar cell arrays, battery packs, charge and discharge controllers, inverters, AC power distribution cabinets, solar tracking control systems and other equipment. It is widely used due to its advantages of easy installation and construction, cleaning and safety. According to the principle of photovoltaic effect, solar power generation devices use solar photovoltaic cells to directly convert solar radiation energy into electrical energy. Under the conditions of sufficient sunlight during the day, the power generation efficiency is high, but under the conditions of night or cloudy days, the solar energy system is basically Without power generation, all-weather power generation cannot be achieved. On the other hand, wind power technology is becoming more and more mature, it is a safe and reliable energy source, and the economy of wind power generation is also increasing. Generator to generate electricity. However, the existing wind power generation device is only suitable for high wind speed areas, and cannot be widely used in low wind speed areas, and needs to be improved.

If the wind power technology and the solar power generation technology are combined, wind power is used to generate power at night and when there is no sunlight on cloudy days, and solar power is used for sunny days. When there is both wind and sun, the two can work at the same time, which can realize all-weather power generation function. At the same time, it is more economical and scientific than single wind and solar power generation. Therefore, how to provide a wind-solar hybrid power generation system and at the same time improve the situation that the existing wind power generation device cannot be applied in low wind speed areas is a technical problem to be solved urgently in the present invention.

In the wind-solar hybrid power generation system provided by the present invention, when the wind acts on the guide vanes of the power turbine, the wind turbine rotates to generate continuous mechanical energy, the mechanical energy is converted into electrical energy by the generator, and the electrical energy is stored in the storage battery; When the solar panel is installed, the solar panel converts the solar radiation energy into electrical energy through the photoelectric effect, and stores the electrical energy in the battery; when it rains, the rainwater collection system is used to collect the rainwater falling on the system, and the collected rainwater can be used to flush the toilet. , watering the road, watering flowers and so on. The power generation system includes a combination of wind power generation, solar power generation and rainwater collection functions. The system has a simple structure, green and safe power generation, and high economic benefits of power generation. It is built on the top of high-rise buildings. The geometry depends on the outline of the building structure. Providing green energy without negative visual impact, the grille setup also eliminates concerns about bird strikes, improves system safety and reduces vibration. The system can be used in urban and remote areas with low wind speed and high wind speed, with strong practicability.
