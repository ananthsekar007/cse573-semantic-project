=== ABSTRACT ===
The invention discloses a method for optimizing the capacity configuration of a hybrid energy storage power source in a wind and solar power generation system, comprising: establishing energy models of a wind power generator set, a photovoltaic solar generator set, a storage battery and a super capacitor respectively; Based on the tolerant hierarchical sequence optimization method, according to the pre-set importance ranking, the one-time investment cost and The optimization objective function and the corresponding optimization constraints are established for the operating cost of the whole life cycle; according to the establishment sequence of the optimization objective function and the optimization constraints, the optimal parameters are obtained by solving in turn to realize the optimization of the capacity configuration of the hybrid energy storage power supply. While maximizing the utilization of wind energy and photovoltaic solar energy resources under the premise of load demand, it greatly reduces the one-time investment cost and the operating cost of the whole life cycle.

With the rapid depletion of traditional petroleum resources and the increasing global warming, researchers and scientists around the world are striving to find green alternative energy sources. However, both wind energy and photovoltaic solar energy are intermittent. In order to ensure the stability and reliability of energy supply, it is usually necessary to set up a suitable energy storage system in the wind and solar power generation system. Generally speaking, in small and medium power generation systems consisting of wind and photovoltaic solar energy, storage batteries are used for energy storage. However, the energy storage power supply composed of batteries has problems such as low life cycle, low power density, limited charging/discharging current, and being easily affected by the environment.

In recent years, supercapacitors have gradually attracted attention due to their advantages of high power density, long life, and high charge-discharge efficiency. However, the energy storage capacity of energy storage power sources composed of supercapacitors only is very limited, and the cost is higher than that of energy storage power sources composed of batteries. much higher. Obviously, there will be certain problems when using batteries or supercapacitors alone as energy storage devices for wind and solar power generation systems, so the hybrid energy storage power supply (including batteries and super capacitors) that combines the advantages of both has become an energy storage device for wind and solar power generation systems. Research direction of energy devices.

In the hybrid energy storage power source of wind and solar power generation system, the optimization of capacity configuration is a difficult problem, which is very important for the reliable operation of wind and solar power generation system at reasonable cost. At present, there are relatively few studies on capacity allocation optimization by domestic and foreign scholars, and the main optimization methods focus on genetic algorithm and particle swarm optimization. Although genetic algorithm is simple and easy to implement and has strong search ability for global solutions, this method belongs to the random category. The algorithm requires multiple operations, requires a large amount of calculation, takes a long time, and cannot obtain a stable solution; although the particle swarm algorithm has fast search speed, few parameters to be adjusted, and high efficiency, it has low convergence accuracy and is easy to fall into local optimum. The shortcomings of the solution.
