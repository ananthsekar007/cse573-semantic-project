=== ABSTRACT ===
The invention provides a black start control method and a system for a wind-solar energy storage new energy power station, which comprise the following steps: when the power grid fails and the wind-solar-energy storage new energy power station has a black start condition, the energy storage system outputs voltage based on a voltage reference value continuously set within a certain time, and the voltage and the frequency of a power supply bus are recovered until the voltage and the frequency of the power supply bus reach rated values; after the voltage and the frequency of the power supply bus reach rated values, the wind turbine generator, the photovoltaic generator and the energy storage battery are controlled to supply power based on the wind-solar-energy storage coordination control method, and stable power supply to loads is achieved.

How to enable the system to quickly recover after a blackout, that is, to achieve black start, has become an important issue for the safe operation of modern power systems.

The so-called black start means that after the entire system is shut down due to a fault, the system is completely powered off (it is not excluded that the isolated small power grid still operates), and it is in a completely "black" state, without relying on the help of other networks, through the system with self-starting ability. The generator set starts, drives the generator set without self-starting ability, gradually expands the scope of system recovery, and finally realizes the recovery of the entire system.

Wind energy and photovoltaic power generation have the advantages of being clean and pollution-free, but at the same time have uncertainty and random volatility.
