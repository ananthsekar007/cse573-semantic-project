The invention relates to a wind-light energy and shore-electricity charged storage-battery electric ship and a control method thereof. The electric ship comprises a catamaran body, wherein an electric propulsion device is respectively connected with a storage battery and a charger, the charger is connected with the storage battery, and an shore-electricity access device is arranged on the charger; a wind power generator and a solar battery plate are connected with the charger through a control desk; an alternating-current square-wave permanent-magnetic brushless direct-current motor controlled by a PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) technology and driven by a phase-decoupling type direct-current variable-frequency stepless speed-regulation controller is adopted in the electric propulsion device; the solar battery plate is also arranged on the catamaran body; a rapidly-chargeable lithium-iron phosphate power storage battery is adopted as the storage battery; and a plurality of temperature control devices are installed in a battery cabin for placing the storage battery.
