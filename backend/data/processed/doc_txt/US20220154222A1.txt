Novel nucleic acid modifiers. The present inventions generally relate to site-specific delivery of nucleic acid modifiers and includes novel DNA-binding proteins and effectors that can be rapidly programmed to make site-specific DNA modifications. The present inventions also provide a synthetic all-in-one genome editor (SAGE) systems comprising designer DNA sequence readers and a set of small molecules that induce double-strand breaks, enhance cellular permeability, inhibit NHEJ and activate HDR, as well as methods of using and delivering such systems. 1. An engineered, non-naturally occurring nucleic acid modifying system, comprising:
a) one or more DNA readers, wherein a first engineered, non-naturally occurring DNA reader, binds a target nucleic acid; and b) one or more effector components, wherein a first effector component is a small molecule and modifies the target nucleic acid.
