The invention discloses a spherical integrated wind-solar hybrid power generation system and method, comprising a wind turbine, a solar photovoltaic panel, a wind-solar hybrid controller, a load, a battery pack and a sensor; The solar photovoltaic panel and the sensor are connected and controlled; the wind-solar hybrid controller is also connected with the battery pack to perform charge and discharge control; the battery pack is connected with the load to supply power. The photovoltaic power generation device and the wind power generation device are reasonably combined, and the solar panel is transformed and fixed on the wind turbine blade, wherein the blade is designed to be arc-shaped and evenly encircled into a spherical structure, so as to realize the integrated power generation of wind energy and solar energy, effectively It solves the problems of low stability of the wind and light single power generation system, large volume and scattered installation of the traditional wind-solar hybrid device, and the wind-solar power generation system is greatly affected by the illumination angle and wind direction. 1.一种球状一体式风光互补发电系统，其特征在于，包括：太阳能光伏板(201)、风力发电机(202)、风光互补控制器(203)、蓄电池组(204)和传感器；所述风光互补控制器(203)分别与所述太阳能光伏板(201)、风力发电机(202)以及所述传感器相连，并对它们进行控制；所述风光互补控制器(203)还与所述蓄电池组(204)相连，对其进行充放电控制；所述蓄电池组(204)与所述负载相连为其供电。1.
