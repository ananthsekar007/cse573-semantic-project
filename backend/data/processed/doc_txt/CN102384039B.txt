The invention provides a hybrid wind-solar hybrid pumped storage system, which includes a wind-solar hybrid unit, a controller, a DC load, an AC load, an inverter, and a pumped storage unit; the wind-solar hybrid unit includes a wind power generator set and a photovoltaic array; the pumped storage unit It includes a reversible water pump turbine unit, an upper pool and a lower pool; the controller includes a wind-solar hybrid controller, a digital signal processor chip, a voltage/current sampling circuit, a voltage/current transformer group, a circuit breaker drive circuit and three circuit breakers device. In the present invention, the traditional wind-solar hybrid system energy storage device—the storage battery pack is replaced by a pumped storage power generation system, and a reversible pump turbine is used in the pumped storage unit to pump water or generate electricity, and the segmental integration method is applied to the pumped storage unit. The pool performs energy monitoring and control.
