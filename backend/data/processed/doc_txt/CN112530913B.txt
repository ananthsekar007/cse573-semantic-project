Packaging structure and manufacturing method thereof. The disclosed embodiments are related to a packaging structure and a manufacturing method thereof. A packaging structure includes a circuit element, a first semiconductor die, a second semiconductor die, a heat dissipation element, and an insulating encapsulation body. The first semiconductor die and the second semiconductor die are located on the circuit element. The heat dissipation element is connected to the first semiconductor die, and the first semiconductor die is located between the circuit element and the heat dissipation element, wherein the sum of the first thickness of the first semiconductor die and the third thickness of the heat dissipation element is substantially equal to the second thickness of the second semiconductor die. The insulating encapsulation body encapsulates the first semiconductor die, the second semiconductor die, and the heat dissipation element, wherein the surface of the heat dissipation element is substantially flush with the insulating encapsulation body. 1.一种封装结构，包括：1.
