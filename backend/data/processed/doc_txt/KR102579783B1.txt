Vision inspection system by using remote learning of product defects image. A vision inspection system and method through remote learning of product defect images are disclosed. The vision inspection system through remote learning of product defect images is connected to cameras, sensors, LED lights, and controllers, and is equipped with a machine vision inspection system equipped with machine vision image analysis software, reads the product ID, and reads the product's camera image data. An edge platform including a defect inspection module that provides shape judgment inspection, foreign matter inspection, or scratch inspection, a defect judgment module, and a manufacturing data transmission module for learning; Middleware connected to the edge platform and interoperable with the service platform; And it is linked with the edge platform through the middleware, provides defect judgment manufacturing intelligence and defect prediction manufacturing intelligence, detects atypical defective images by comparing them with accumulated and stored defect image learning data, and detects the presence or absence of foreign substances in the camera image data of the product.
