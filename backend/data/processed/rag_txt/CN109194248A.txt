=== ABSTRACT ===
The invention discloses a spherical integrated wind-solar hybrid power generation system and method, comprising a wind turbine, a solar photovoltaic panel, a wind-solar hybrid controller, a load, a battery pack and a sensor; The solar photovoltaic panel and the sensor are connected and controlled; the wind-solar hybrid controller is also connected with the battery pack to perform charge and discharge control; the battery pack is connected with the load to supply power. The photovoltaic power generation device and the wind power generation device are reasonably combined, and the solar panel is transformed and fixed on the wind turbine blade, wherein the blade is designed to be arc-shaped and evenly encircled into a spherical structure, so as to realize the integrated power generation of wind energy and solar energy, effectively It solves the problems of low stability of the wind and light single power generation system, large volume and scattered installation of the traditional wind-solar hybrid device, and the wind-solar power generation system is greatly affected by the illumination angle and wind direction.
