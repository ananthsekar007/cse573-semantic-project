=== ABSTRACT ===
The present application discloses a video processing method, a machine learning model training method, and related devices and equipment. The method relates to artificial intelligence and includes: extracting features from a video to be processed to obtain a video feature vector; extracting features from a text to be processed to obtain a text feature vector; corresponding the text to be processed to the video to be processed; concatenating the text feature vector with the video feature vector to obtain a multimodal feature vector; fusing the multimodal feature vector to obtain a fused feature vector; and classifying the video to be processed based on the fused feature vector to obtain a label for the video to be processed. This method can improve the degree of fusion of video information and text information, allowing the two to complement each other and improve the accuracy of video label recognition, thereby facilitating a better understanding of the subject of the video.

Video processing method, machine learning model training method, related device and equipment
Technical Field
The present application relates to the field of artificial intelligence technologies, and in particular, to a video processing method, a machine learning model training method, and related devices and apparatuses.
Background
In recent years, a video information stream is popular worldwide, and compared with the traditional mode of information transmission by text, the video information stream has the advantages of more abundant information quantity, more convenient browsing mode, more impact force and the like.
Generally, the sources of video are more from major broadcasters who frequently distribute video on small daily videos such as self-media, e.g., fast hands, tremble, etc., than professional video editors. Compared with text information, small video content released from media is richer and more diverse in form, so how to understand the subject of video in an efficient way becomes a technical difficulty. For small videos, we want to summarize the video information through a series of tags (tags) to facilitate locating the subject of the video, thereby helping the upper business party to complete the distribution of the video content.
For labeling of small video labels, it is often the case that the small video is labeled from a text perspective or a visual perspective, respectively. However, labeling a small video only depends on visual information or text information, and there is a problem of inaccurate labeling.
Disclosure of Invention
The embodiment of the application provides a video processing method, a machine learning model training method, a related device and equipment, which can improve the fusion degree of video information and text information, so that the video information and the text information complement each other, and improve the accuracy of label identification of a video, thereby facilitating better understanding of the theme of the video.
The first aspect of the embodiment of the application discloses a video processing method, which comprises the steps of extracting features from a video to be processed to obtain a video feature vector, extracting features from a text to be processed to obtain a text feature vector, enabling the text to be processed to correspond to the video to be processed, splicing the text feature vector and the video feature vector to obtain a multi-mode feature vector, carrying out feature fusion on the multi-mode feature vector to obtain a fusion feature vector, and classifying the video to be processed based on the fusion feature vector to obtain a label of the video to be processed.
By the method, the server can better fuse the video information and the text information, so that the video information and the text information complement each other, the accuracy of label identification of the video is improved, and the theme of the video can be better understood. The method comprises the steps of obtaining a video theme, wherein the text information is utilized to effectively promote model identification from a perception level to a cognition level, so that a label most relevant to the video theme is obtained.

Modality (modality) the source or form of each type of information may be referred to as a modality. (3) Neural network
Neural Networks (NN) are artificial neural networks (ARTIFICIAL NEURAL NETWORK, ANN), abbreviated as neural networks or neural-like networks, which are mathematical or computational models that mimic the structure and function of biological neural networks (the central nervous system of animals, particularly the brain) in the field of machine learning and cognitive sciences, and are used to estimate or approximate functions.
   Language model training method
The language model training method (bidirectional encoder representations from transformers, BERT) is a training method utilizing massive texts, and is widely applied to various natural language processing tasks, such as text classification, text matching, machine reading understanding and the like.
 ¡ Model parameters
Model parameters are one number of relationships between functions and variables that are established using common variables. In artificial neural networks, the model parameters are typically real matrices.
 ¢ Model training
Model training, namely multi-classification learning is carried out on the image data set. The model can be constructed by adopting deep learning frameworks such as Tensor Flow and torch, and a multi-classification model is formed by using multi-layer combination of neural network layers such as CNN. The input of the model is a three-channel or original channel matrix formed by reading an image through tools such as openCV, the model is output as multi-classification probability, and the webpage category is finally output through algorithms such as softmax. During training, the model approaches to the correct trend through an objective function such as cross entropy and the like.
  Convolutional neural network
Convolutional neural networks (convolutional neural networks, CNN) are a class of feedforward neural networks (feed forward neural networks) that contain convolutional computations and have a deep structure, which are one of the representative algorithms for deep learning (DEEPLEARNING). The convolutional neural network has a token learning (representation learning) capability, and can carry out translation-invariant classification (shift-invariantclassification) on input information according to a hierarchical structure of the convolutional neural network.
 ¤ Circulating neural network
A recurrent neural network (recurrent neural network, RNN) is a class of neural networks used to process sequence data. It differs from other neural networks in that RNNs can better process sequential information, i.e., recognize that there is a relationship between the inputs before and after. In NLP, it is obviously not enough to understand a whole sentence, and to understand the words that make up the sentence in isolation, we need to process the whole sequence linked by these words in its entirety.
  Long-short term memory network
The long and short term memory neural network (long short term networks, LSTMs) is a special cyclic neural network capable of capturing long term dependencies. Is specifically designed to avoid long-term dependency problems. Remembering information for a long time has become its default behavior and does not require specialized learning.
 ¦ Bidirectional attention neural network
The model architecture of the bi-directional attention neural network (bidirectional encoder representation from transformers, BERT) is based on multi-layer bi-directional transform decoding, because the decoder is not able to obtain the information to be predicted, the main innovation of the model is on the pre-training pre-traing method, namely, the two methods Masked LM and Next Sentence Prediction are used to capture the characteristics of words and sentences respectively.
Where "bi-directional" means that the model can use both the preceding and following words of information when processing a word, the source of this "bi-directional" is that the BERT differs from the traditional language model in that it does not predict the most likely current word under all the preceding words, but randomly masks some words and uses all the unmasked words for prediction.
  Depth residual network
The depth residual error network (deep residual network, resNet) adopts a jump structure as a basic structure of the network, so that the problem that the learning efficiency is low and the accuracy cannot be effectively improved due to deepening of the network (also called network degradation) can be solved. By superimposing the layers of y=x (called IDENTITY MAPPINGS, identity mapping) on a shallow network basis, the network can be made to increase with depth without degradation.
 ¨ Encoder-decoder
The encoder-decoder architecture is a network architecture commonly used in machine translation technology. The method comprises two parts of an encoder and a decoder, wherein the encoder converts input text into a series of context vectors capable of expressing input text characteristics, and the decoder receives the output result of the encoder as own input and outputs a corresponding text sequence in another language.

And S604, the server performs feature fusion on the multi-mode feature vectors to obtain fusion feature vectors.
In some embodiments, the server may input the multimodal feature vector to a fusion module to obtain a fused feature vector. The training method of the fusion module may be referred to the related description in the first embodiment, and will not be repeated here.
The fusion module may be a convolutional neural network, which may include at least one convolutional layer and at least one pooling layer. The convolution layer is used for extracting the characteristics of the multi-mode characteristic vector to obtain an initial characteristic vector, and the pooling layer is used for sampling the initial characteristic vector to obtain a more accurate fusion characteristic vector. Both the convolution layer and the pooling layer include an activation function.
Specifically, the server inputs the multi-modal feature vector to the convolution layer, and carries out convolution operation by using the convolution kernel and the multi-modal feature vector, that is, carries out inner product operation on the multi-modal feature vector and the convolution kernel, so as to obtain a convolution result corresponding to the multi-modal feature vector. Next, the convolution result is non-linearly transformed based on the activation function and a bias vector is added to obtain an initial feature vector. And thirdly, inputting the initial feature vector into a pooling layer, and performing feature sampling on the initial feature vector. Then, the convolution result is subjected to nonlinear transformation based on the activation function, and a bias vector is added to obtain a fusion feature vector.
In other embodiments, the server may obtain the fusion feature vector in other manners, and the fusion module may be other network models such as a recurrent neural network, a deep residual network, and the like. The application is not limited in this regard.
S605, the server classifies the videos to be processed based on the fusion feature vector to obtain labels of the videos to be processed.
In some embodiments, the server may input the fused feature vector to a classification module to obtain a label of the video to be processed. The classification module may include at least one fully connected layer that may classify the lines to be processed to obtain tags for the video to be processed. In addition, the fully connected layer includes an activation function including a weight matrix and a bias constant. The training method of the classification module may be referred to the related description in the first embodiment, and will not be repeated here.
Specifically, the server may input the fused feature vector to the full connection layer, and perform nonlinear transformation on the fused feature vector based on the weight matrix and the bias vector of the activation function, to obtain a prediction score of each feature in the fused feature vector. The feature may be marked as a primary feature when the predictive score of each feature is greater than a first threshold, e.g., the first threshold may take 60 points, and the primary feature may be normalized to obtain a predictive probability that each feature in the video to be processed is a label of the video to be processed, and the feature may be determined as a label of the video to be processed when the predictive probability of the feature is greater than a second threshold, e.g., the second threshold may take 0.5 points.
For example, the fusion feature vector contains five features of pig's trotters, beauty, food, table and water cup, wherein the prediction scores of the five features are 80 points, 60 points, 70 points, 50 points and 40 points respectively. If the first threshold is 60 minutes, after the prediction scores of the five features are respectively compared with the first threshold, the pig trotter, the food and the beauty can be determined to be main features, wherein the prediction probabilities of the three main features are respectively 0.8, 0.7 and 0.6, and if the second threshold is 0.5, after the prediction probabilities of the three main features are respectively compared with the second threshold, the pig trotter, the food and the beauty can be determined to be the labels of the videos to be processed.
It should be understood that N and M in the first and second embodiments may be different.
