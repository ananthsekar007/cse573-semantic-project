=== ABSTRACT ===
A monitoring method of a wind-solar-storage integrated micro-grid capable of being operated in a grid-connected mode can predict the power generation power of wind-solar power generation equipment in the micro-grid and the load change in the micro-grid, track voltage information of grid-connected points of a large power grid, acquire a large power grid dispatching instruction in real time, detect the battery capacity of a storage battery module in real time, set a discharge interval of an energy storage system, optimally manage the energy of the energy storage system based on an SOC hierarchical control strategy, correct the charge-discharge power of the energy storage system in real time, optimize the working performance of the energy storage system, make and implement an optimal control strategy, ensure that the micro-grid participates in voltage regulation of the large power grid according to the requirements of the large power grid when in grid-.
