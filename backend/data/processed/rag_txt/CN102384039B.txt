=== ABSTRACT ===
The invention provides a hybrid wind-solar hybrid pumped storage system, which includes a wind-solar hybrid unit, a controller, a DC load, an AC load, an inverter, and a pumped storage unit; the wind-solar hybrid unit includes a wind power generator set and a photovoltaic array; the pumped storage unit It includes a reversible water pump turbine unit, an upper pool and a lower pool; the controller includes a wind-solar hybrid controller, a digital signal processor chip, a voltage/current sampling circuit, a voltage/current transformer group, a circuit breaker drive circuit and three circuit breakers device. In the present invention, the traditional wind-solar hybrid system energy storage device—the storage battery pack is replaced by a pumped storage power generation system, and a reversible pump turbine is used in the pumped storage unit to pump water or generate electricity, and the segmental integration method is applied to the pumped storage unit. The pool performs energy monitoring and control. The invention solves the disadvantages of high price, short service life, environmental pollution and the like of the storage battery pack, improves system stability and power supply reliability, and reduces system construction and operation costs.

At present, energy conservation, emission reduction, green energy, and sustainable development have become the focus of attention of all countries. The ever-increasing price of fossil fuels, the increasingly prominent issue of the greenhouse effect, and the increasing demand for energy have made people's enthusiasm for research on new energy applications unprecedentedly high. Wind energy and solar energy resources have been favored by many researchers as a clean and efficient renewable energy. However, wind energy and solar energy resources also have the characteristics of instability and discontinuity. The emergence of wind and solar hybrid systems has solved this shortcoming of wind and solar resources to a certain extent. At the same time, the application of storage devices in wind and solar hybrid systems has further improved Increased system stability. In the wind-solar hybrid system, the traditional electric energy storage and transposition mostly use battery packs, but the battery packs have the characteristics of high cost, short life, difficult maintenance, and possible acid leakage of the battery, which may cause environmental pollution, which increases the operating cost of the system and affects system reliability.

The hybrid wind-solar hybrid pumped storage system of the present invention includes a wind-solar hybrid unit, a controller, a DC load, an AC load, an inverter and a pumped storage unit, wherein the wind-solar hybrid unit includes a wind power generator set and a photovoltaic array, and the pumped storage unit includes Reversible pump-turbine unit, upper and lower basins. The pump-turbine unit is placed between the upper and lower pools. When the pumped energy storage unit is in the power generation state, water is released from the upper pool to impact the pump-turbine unit, and the water flows into the lower pool. When pumping water, the pump-turbine unit pumps water from the lower pool to the upper pool. In the pumped energy storage unit, the device for pumping water from the upper pool and using the water in the upper pool to generate electricity adopts the same equipment reversible pump turbine; the reversible pump turbine set used in the pumped energy storage unit includes DC Reversible pump-turbine and AC reversible pump-turbine can use DC power supply to drive DC reversible pump-turbine to pump water, and can use AC power to drive AC reversible pump-turbine to pump water. DC reversible pump can be used for power generation The hydraulic turbine unit generates DC power to meet the DC load demand, and can use the AC reversible pump turbine to generate AC power to meet the AC load demand.
